COVID-19 Mortality Rate

The rate of deaths per country from COIVD-19

These percentages are specific to how each country is testing. Often those with mild cases are not being counted for, as a result, those countries limiting testing will appear to have a higher mortality rate. The US had just begun testing for the virus, at the time these numbers were produced. As more folks are tested the rate will likely drop as more people who have it will recover. Although the lack of healthcare has yet to be factored into how people are treated in the US. Overall everyone is probably going to be fine, healthy, young able-bodied people will likely experience something similar to the flu at worse. the focus should be on protecting the elderly and those with prior illnesses.
